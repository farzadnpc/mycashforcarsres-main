Toyota Wrecking FAQs

Do you buy Toyotas with missing engines?

Yes! Even if the engine is blown, removed, or completely missing, the chassis, panels, and interior components of your Toyota still carry value for us.

How fast can you remove my Toyota?

We operate 24/7 across Victoria. If you call us in the morning, we can usually provide same-day collection and payment.

Do I need to clean the car before you arrive?

Not at all. You can leave your old Toyota exactly as it is. Just remember to remove your personal belongings, e-tags, and license plates before we tow it away.
