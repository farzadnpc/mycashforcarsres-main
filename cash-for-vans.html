The Heavy Wrecking Process

Once your commercial vehicle arrives at our Dandenong South yard, it undergoes a meticulous dismantling process.

  • Fluid Extraction: Heavy trucks contain massive amounts of hydraulic fluids, diesel, and oil. We safely drain these to prevent environmental hazards.
  • Parts Salvage: We carefully remove working engines, gearboxes, and heavy axles to sell to local transport companies.
  • Industrial Crushing: The remaining massive steel frame is compacted and sent to metal refineries for eco-friendly recycling.
