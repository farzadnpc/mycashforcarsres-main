From the moment you contact us to the final parts sale, here is how our premium wrecking service operates.

2. Expert Audi Wreckers

As premier Audi wreckers in Victoria, our facility is heavily equipped to handle the complex electronics and dense frames of German luxury cars. We safely transport your broken vehicle back to our yard at zero cost to you.

Our specialists then begin the meticulous process of dismantling the car, ensuring no valuable components are destroyed in the teardown.

3. Eco-Friendly Auto Recycling

We pride ourselves on our safe car recycling program. Audis contain various specific synthetic oils, coolants, and heavy metals. Our team meticulously drains all hazardous fluids to prevent soil contamination.

Once the car is stripped of parts, we process the remaining steel and aluminum so it can be smelted and reused in future manufacturing. Sell to us and keep Victoria green.

4. Genuine Used Audi Parts

Buying brand-new parts from an Audi dealership is incredibly expensive. Our wrecking process allows us to salvage and test thousands of genuine used Audi parts.

If you are a mechanic or a DIY repairer, we supply everything from Q7 engine blocks to A4 headlights and interior leathers at a fraction of the retail price.
