Heavy Vehicle Eco-Recycling

Disposing of massive commercial vehicles comes with significant environmental responsibilities. Hino trucks hold vast amounts of industrial chemicals that cannot be allowed into Victorian soil or waterways.

  • Depollution: We meticulously drain heavy diesel tanks, engine oils, differentials, and coolant systems.
  • Material Separation: Specialized commercial tires and large batteries are removed for distinct recycling streams.
  • Steel Crushing: The remaining massive steel cab and chassis are crushed and melted down for reuse in modern manufacturing.

By selling your Hino to us, you are guaranteed a fully compliant, green disposal process.
