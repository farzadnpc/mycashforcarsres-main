Bring your driver's licence and proof of ownership. If rego is in another name, a signed authority is needed. We supply a receipt and help with VicRoads disposal notices.

How do you pay?

We pay via instant OSKO bank transfer in compliance with Victorian regulations. Business cheque available on request for commercial sellers.

Is towing really free?

Yes — always. Free across Melbourne and most regional centres. Remote pickups arranged with notice.

Will you buy an unregistered or damaged car?

Absolutely. We specialise in unregistered, accident-damaged and non-running vehicles. Tell us the condition for the best quote.
