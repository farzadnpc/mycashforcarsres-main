Frequently asked questions

Answers to common questions about selling your car for cash.

What documents do I need?

Bring your driver licence or photo ID and proof of ownership. If the rego is in another name, provide a signed authority. We can supply a receipt and help with VicRoads disposal notices.

How do you pay?

We pay via instant OSKO bank transfer in compliance with Victorian regulations. Business cheque is available on request for commercial sellers.

Do you charge for towing?

No. Towing is free across Melbourne and most regional centres. Remote pickups can be arranged with notice.

Will you buy an unregistered or damaged car?

Absolutely. We specialise in unregistered, accident damaged and non-running vehicles. Tell us about the condition for the best quote.
