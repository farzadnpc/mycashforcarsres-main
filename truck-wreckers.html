Eco-Conscious Truck Recycling

Disposing of massive commercial vehicles presents severe environmental risks if handled incorrectly. A single heavy truck can contain hundreds of liters of toxic fluids.

  • Strict Depollution: Our team systematically drains massive diesel tanks, engine oils, differentials, and air conditioning gases to prevent soil contamination.
  • Industrial Crushing: Once the fluids and hazardous heavy-duty batteries are removed, the immense steel cab and chassis are crushed.
  • Sustainable Foundry Supply: The compacted heavy steel is sent directly to local metal foundries, severely reducing the need for raw ore mining in Australia.
